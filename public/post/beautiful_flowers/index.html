Beautiful flowers

21 Mar, 2018 · Read in about 1 min · (60 words) · Share this on:
Poem

Thousands of beautiful flowers,
still can’t overcome my scars.

New opportunities everyday,
but can’t forget the lost fray.

Going off with a beautiful smile,
but who knows about the sorrows pile.

Love to see the happiness around,
but is somehow limited by bounds.

Still hopeful what the life may bring,
let me break the tied strings.
