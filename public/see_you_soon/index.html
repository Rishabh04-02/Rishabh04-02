See you soon

20 May, 2018 · Read in about 1 min · (61 words) · Share this on:
Poem

See you soon,
even if that means passing through dunes.

You are one of my dearest friends,
for whom I pray, her happiness ascends.

You are one of the finest persons in my life,
whom I never want to see in strife.

You are the one inspiring girl,
who one day will surely shine like a pearl.
